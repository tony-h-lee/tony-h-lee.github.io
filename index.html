Stubs

This is a CRUD app developed by myself and four other students for a group project. Users can search for group members based on criteria as well as an option to join a group of randomly selected users within a first come first serve queue.


It is a single-page application developed with NodeJS and the Express framework. Backend data storage is handled by MongoDB and the Mongoose module for NodeJS. The queue system is built from the socket.io module.

Legacy of the Sun

An online card game between two players. Constructed with NodeJS and primarily uses AJAX and socket events for client-server synchronization. This is the project that showed me the disadvantages of not utilizing frameworks such as AngularJS, React, or VueJS, as the data-binding would have came in handy when trying to maintain transparency of the client-server interaction for users.


Implements responsive design and can be played on various mobile devices.
